Wendy Stoffels
Office Manager

Wendy Stoffels advises the Chief Executive Officer, Chief Financial Officer, Chief Operating Officer and Fellows of the Shuttleworth Foundation with regards to proposals for funding. All proposals received by the Foundation are channelled to the team via her.

Wendy encourages enthusiasm amongst the team and generates ideas on improving the smooth running of the Shuttleworth Foundation office. She also ensures deadlines are met.

Wendy has nearly forty years of experience in working with non-governmental organisations, including 15 years spent at the University of the Western Cape. She has worked with communities for many years and has a strong background in administration, supervising, finance, coordinating events and international conferences. For many years she coordinated foreign students doing their doctorates in South Africa.
