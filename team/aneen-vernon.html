Aneen Vernon
Financial Accountant

Aneen Vernon joined the Shuttleworth Foundation as Financial Accountant in March 2013, after spending 7 years in Malawi fulfilling Financial and Operations Management roles of international import/export companies.

Her role in the Foundation is the daily management of and reporting on Fellowship Funding. Aneen holds a B.Com degree from the University of Stellenbosch and has a wealth of business management experience in a variety of industries covering retail, mining, manufacturing, legal, security and shipping.

She is passionate about her family, enjoys traveling and reading, and always welcomes a new challenge.
